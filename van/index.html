NeuroTechVAN

The International NeuroTech Network.
From Hackers to Experts.

We are a group of enthusiasts, hackers, researchers and entrepreneurs sharing our passion for neurotechnology in Vancouver.







Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about neurotechnology and promote innovation in the related fields through teaching, mentorship and networking.

Educate

NeuroTechVAN holds various events, such as regular meetups and workshops, on topics that are of particular interest to the BCI community as well as the general public. Together with experts and enthusiasts, we teach and spread the word about neurotechnology, from future perspectives to technical state-of-the-art.

Our Team

Benjamin Tam
Executive Director

To pioneer innovation in neuroscience and machine learning, Ben founded a brain computer interface firm called Somatic Technologies. He also manages a hedge fund, Arreat Investments Corporation, where he focuses on quantitative financial engineering, equity investing, and venture capital acquisitions. His experience in business and technology startups give him a unique perspective that focuses on helping entrepreneurs create innovative and revolutionary companies. When not tinkering in STEM fields, Ben enjoys hunting, martial arts, philosophy, good music, and fantasizing about having telekinesis. He believes that we can use technological progress to forever solve the world's greatest problems and he is looking for like minded folks to join him on this audacious mission.
